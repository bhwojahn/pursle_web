privacy

last updated: april 2026. pursle ("we") runs this site to explain what we build and to receive inbound project inquiries.

what we collect

if you use the contact form, we collect the name, company, email address, and the text you enter in "what's slowing you down?" we do not ask for a phone number or social profiles in v1.

why we collect it

we use that information to respond to you and to keep a record of the inquiry in our database. we may send a single internal notification email to our team when a form is submitted.

where it is stored

submissions are stored in a postgresql database on infrastructure we control. the database is not exposed to the public internet. access is limited to what is needed to operate the site.

retention

we keep lead records for as long as needed to follow up and work together, unless you ask us to delete them sooner. if you want deletion, email us from the address you used on the form and we will remove your row where technically feasible.

cookies and similar

the contact flow may set a short-lived, strict same-site cookie used to prevent cross-site request forgery. it is not used for advertising.

international visitors

if you are in the eu, uk, or brazil, you have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data we hold. contact us using the same channels as on the main site.

changes

we will update this page when practices change. the date at the top will move when we edit it.
